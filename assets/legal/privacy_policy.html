Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

TagMe ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that connects university students in Bangladesh for ride-sharing. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Profile information: Name, university, gender, and profile photo. This data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage.
  • Location data: Device GPS location while the app is in the foreground ONLY. We do NOT collect location data in the background. Your location is never tracked when the app is closed.
  • Ride data: Origin, destination, departure time, transport type, and seat availability for rides you create or join.
  • Chat messages: Messages exchanged with ride-sharing partners through the in-app chat feature.
  • Device tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens used to deliver push notifications to your device.
  • Fare data: Fare estimates and split records between co-riders for ride cost sharing.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Display your location on the map to nearby students so they can find potential ride-sharing partners.
  • Match you with compatible ride-sharing partners based on route proximity, departure time, and transport preferences.
  • Enable in-app chat with matched riders for ride coordination.
  • Send push notifications for ride matches, chat messages, and departure reminders.
  • Calculate and track fare estimates and splits between co-riders.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) servers.
  • Profile photos are stored in Firebase Storage.
  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

Data Sharing

  • We do NOT sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties.
  • Your profile information (name, university, photo) is visible to other TagMe users on the map and in ride listings.
  • Your chat messages are visible only to the participants of that conversation.
  • We use Google Firebase services, which may process data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Location Data

TagMe accesses your location ONLY while the app is open and in the foreground. We do NOT access your location when the app is closed or running in the background.

  • Location is used to show nearby students on the map and to match ride routes.
  • We request the following Android permissions: ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION.
  • We do NOT request or use ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION. Your location is never collected in the background.

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as you actively use the app and maintain an account.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.
  • Upon receiving a deletion request, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

TagMe is intended for university and college students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
